Snow Leopard

The Snow Leopard is on the endangered species list with global populations estimated to be between 4,080 and 8,700.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera uncia
  • Average Length: 155cm-250cm (nose to tail)
  • Average Lifespan: 15-18 years
  • Habitat: Central and South Asia Mountains

The Snow Leopard has grey fur with a white under layer. Unlike most other large cats it has green or grey eyes and an extraordinarily long tail.
